# Session 4 — Thursday 8 May 2026

**Phase:** Rehab — glute chain and knee tracking  
**Goal:** Progress clamshell and hip abduction to 3 sets. Increase lateral band walk volume. Consolidate step-down. Introduce single-leg RDL as hip hinge and posterior chain work.  
**Duration:** 60–75 min  
**Stop if:** Knee pain > 3/10, any sharp or locking sensation, diastasis doming/coning, or breathing distress

## Block 2 — Knee load (15 min)

*Only proceed if knee ≤ 3/10 after Block 1.*

| Exercise | Sets × Reps | Load | Cues | Demo | Done |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Step-down | 3 × 8 each leg | Bodyweight | 3 sec lower, drive back up with control. Right leg standing. Knee tracks over second toe — no caving. This is the main knee exercise now; step-up is dropped this session to give step-down the focus it needs. | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HIUL9MpxTho | [ ] |
| Single-leg RDL (new) | 2 × 8 each side | Bodyweight, hand on bar for balance | Stand on one leg, soft knee. Hinge forward at the hip, free leg floats back as a counterbalance. Lower until mild hamstring pull — don't chase depth. Spine stays neutral throughout. Use the bar lightly for balance — the goal is hip hinge pattern, not balance challenge yet. Right leg first. | https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gz9l8UA_KXs | [ ] |
