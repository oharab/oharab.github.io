# Session 3 — Monday 5 May 2026

**Phase:** Rehab — glute chain and knee tracking  
**Goal:** Consolidate single-leg glute bridge. Introduce lateral band walk for closed-chain glute med work. Progress step-up to step-down. Add dead bug arm+leg if session 2 went well.  
**Duration:** 60–75 min  
**Stop if:** Knee pain > 3/10, any sharp or locking sensation, diastasis doming/coning, or breathing distress
