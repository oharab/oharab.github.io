# Session 1 — Thursday 30 April 2026

**Phase:** Rehab / movement screen  
**Goal:** Baseline screen, identify knee and diastasis limits, establish starting loads  
**Duration:** 60–75 min  
**Stop if:** Knee pain > 3/10, any sharp or locking sensation, diastasis doming/coning, or breathing distress

---

## Warm-up — 10 min

| Exercise | Sets × Reps | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Diaphragmatic breathing | 1 × 10 breaths | Lying on back, knees bent. Breathe into belly and sides — not chest. Sets the tone for diastasis work. |
| Cat-cow | 1 × 10 | Slow, full range. Note any stiffness. |
| Hip 90/90 switch | 1 × 10 each side | Sit on floor, both legs at 90°. Rotate side to side. Note any hip restriction. |
| Calf raise (bilateral, slow) | 1 × 15 | Bodyweight only. On a step if available. Assess calf/ankle stiffness. |

---

## Block 1 — Glute & hip screen (20 min)

*Purpose: find out where the weak links are in the chain driving the knee tracking issue.*

| Exercise | Sets × Reps | Load | Cues |
|---|---|---|---|
| Glute bridge | 2 × 10 | Bodyweight | Feet hip-width, drive through heels. Note if one glute fires less. Pause 2 sec at top. |
| Clamshell | 2 × 12 each side | Light band or bodyweight | Side-lying, feet together. Rotate top knee up without rolling the pelvis back. Note asymmetry. |
| Side-lying hip abduction | 2 × 12 each side | Bodyweight | Straight top leg, slight forward lean of the foot. No hip hike. |
| Standing hip hinge (RDL pattern) | 2 × 8 | Bodyweight only | Feet hip-width, soft knee, hinge at hip until mild hamstring pull. No rounding of lower back. Stop here — assess knee comfort. |

## Block 2 — Knee load screen (15 min)

*Only proceed if knee ≤ 3/10 after Block 1. If above, skip to Block 3.*

| Exercise | Sets × Reps | Load | Cues |
|---|---|---|---|
| Wall sit | 2 × 20 sec | Bodyweight | Back flat against wall, thighs parallel or above parallel. Stop if pain increases during the hold. |
| Step-up (low box / first step) | 2 × 8 each leg | Bodyweight | Lead with right leg first. Drive through the heel. Observe knee tracking over toes. |
| Slow bodyweight squat | 2 × 8 | Bodyweight | Feet shoulder-width, toes slightly out. Descend to comfortable depth only — do not chase depth. Note where knee pain starts (if at all). |

**After this block, rate right knee:** \_\_ / 10

---

## Block 3 — Diastasis baseline (10 min)

*Self-check before any loaded core work. This is assessment, not training.*

| Check | What to do | What to note |
|---|---|---|
| Lying heel slide | On back, slowly slide one heel along floor. Note any doming or coning at midline. | Present / absent. Which side worse. |
| Dead bug (single arm only) | On back, extend one arm overhead slowly. Does midline bulge? | Present / absent. |
| Calf raise breathing | Standing. Exhale and rise onto toes. Any downward pressure felt? | Present / absent. |

**Note:** Do not progress to loaded core work until diastasis status is clearer. Log observations carefully for the physio.

---

## Cool-down — 10 min

| Exercise | Duration | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Supine figure-4 stretch | 60 sec each side | Glute / hip external rotator. Gentle — no forcing range. |
| Prone hip flexor stretch | 60 sec each side | Half-kneeling if supine is uncomfortable. |
| Seated hamstring stretch | 60 sec each side | Slight forward lean only — no rounding of back. |
| Diaphragmatic breathing | 5 slow breaths | Same as warm-up. End where you started. |
